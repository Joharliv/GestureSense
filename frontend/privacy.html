Privacy policy

Privacy First.

Your gestures stay yours.

01

Camera access

GestureSense uses your webcam for real-time gesture detection. The camera is only active while you're using the app, and you can stop the stream at any time with a single click.

02

Local processing

Whenever possible, gesture processing happens locally on your device. Webcam frames are never stored, never uploaded, and never shared with third parties.

03

Data collection

We do not sell or share camera data — full stop. If you give us your email, it's used only to send product updates and reply to your messages. Unsubscribe anytime.

04

Security

We're committed to secure, privacy-focused AI. Connections are encrypted in transit, models are audited for bias, and access to any account data is strictly limited.
