About GestureSense

Turning gestures into conversations with the power of AI.

Our mission

Break the silence between intent and understanding.

GestureSense helps break communication barriers using AI-powered gesture recognition and real-time speech generation — so anyone, anywhere, can be heard.

How it works

From a hand in the air to a sentence spoken aloud.

01 / Detect

Webcam-based capture

Your camera streams frames straight into the on-device pipeline — no cloud round-trip.

02 / Predict

AI gesture prediction

A trained model classifies static and motion gestures frame by frame.

03 / Build

Sentence building

Smart suggestions stitch gestures into natural, grammatical sentences.

04 / Speak

Text-to-speech

The sentence is spoken aloud in real time with a clear, expressive voice.
